Next Level Chair
SKU: CH100

Description

Each chair is padded with 2-1/2" thick, 6lb. rebond foam covered in 30 oz. vinyl and a wooden smartboard bottom. Maximum artwork size for chair seat is 14" wide x 15" high. Each chair leg has a rubber cap end to protect floors from being scratched. An additional plastic insert is also included for extra floor protection. The chair backrest is 1" closed-celled covered in 30oz. vinyl. Included in the cost of the chair is a 2 color screen print of your school name, mascot, or logo. Maximum screen dimension is 5" high x 13" wide. Two color logo in two locations included with purchase of chair. Artwork locations include chair back, chair bottom, and chair bottom front. Chair front print size is 2" x 10".
