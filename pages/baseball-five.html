Pitching Target 6' x 7' (with frame)
SKU: 360PT-2

Description

Description: 40 oz black vinyl. Effective Velocity, by definition, is the study of pitch speed and how location changes the reaction time by forcing the hitter to hit the ball at a contact point that is different than they were ready for. When a pitcher throws the ball "inside" or "outside", the speed of the pitch changes because the hitter needs to adjust to the location. A pitch can change up to 3 MPH for every 6" inches that the ball changes laterally. The 360 degree Pitching Target helps a pitcher with location. thus fine tuning his Effective Velocity.
