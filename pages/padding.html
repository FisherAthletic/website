Non-Folding Removable Wall Pads
SKU: WM224062

Description

Our most affordable wall mat offers protection and value. Hang the mats on the wall for protection in your gym or remove them for use as exercise mats. They are easy to mount and remove and are available in 15 colors (18 oz. vinyl) and 13 colors (14 oz. vinyl). Fisher WM series wall mats are manufactured from 2" 100 lb. ILD polyfoam with a vinyl "pipe loop" on the top of the mat. Just go to your local hardware store and buy a length of steel pipe (1/2" to 3/4") and fasten the mat to the wall using the SMH1 mat hanging straps included with your mat. Provide your own artwork or choose from our clip art selection.
