Pitching target 5' x 6' (no frame)
SKU: 360PT-1

Description

40 oz black vinyl. Effective Velocity, by definition, is the study of pitch speed and how location changes the reaction time by forcing the hitter to hit the ball at a contact point that is different than they were ready for. When a pitcher throws the ball "inside" or "outside", the speed of the pitch changes because the hitter needs to adjust to the location. A pitch can change up to 3 MPH for every 6" inches that the ball changes laterally. The 360 degree Pitching Target helps a pitcher with location. thus fine tuning his Effective Velocity.
