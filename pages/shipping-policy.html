Shipping Policy

Fisher Athletic will use its ground carrier of choice for ground shipments. Shipments that must be shipped via Freight will be awarded to the company that provides the best pricing. The customer is responsible for all shipping charges unless there was a written contract between the customer and Fisher Athletic that depicts otherwise.

Shipments of Fisher products leave the factory in perfect condition. If a product is delivered short or in a damaged condition, it is the responsibility of the delivering carrier. Customers receiving shipments should carefully inspect the cartons for damage before signing the delivery receipt. All shortages and damage should be noted on the delivery receipt before signing for the shipment.

If a product is found to be damaged after being removed from its box, notify Fisher Athletic and the delivering carrier immediately. Ask the carrier to fill out a "Concealed Damage Report". Normally, there is a 10-day grace period within which to file a claim.
Freight Claims: Fisher Athletic Equipment is not responsible for lost or damaged freight. Freight claims must be filed against the delivering carrier. Replacement merchandise will be sent only after the freight claim is accepted by the delivering carrier.

If a shipment is refused for any reason, and the merchandise is returned to the factory, the customer will be charged for all freight and a re-stocking charge of 20%.

Allow 4 weeks for delivery. Fisher Athletic does not guarantee delivery time. Order before June 1st to avoid the pre-season rush.

Expedited shipping is available upon the customer's request. The customer is responsible for all costs that are associated with expedited shipping.

Call customer service for freight estimates. Fisher Athletic attempts to ship all orders at the lowest possible freight cost. Freight quotes are subject to change without notice. Fisher Athletic is not responsible for differences between estimated and actual freight charges. Residential delivery will incur an additional freight charge.
